Introduction to D's Artsy Gallery

I am a highschool student who found interest in sketching early 2020. I looked for new ways to improve and further my interest.

I started taking online classes since the beginining of summer break. As i am improving, I do not want this talent to go to waste. That's why I opened an Instagram account dedicated to my art works as well as a YouTube Channel and an Etsy Account. It all came step by step.I may not be the sharpest knife in the drawer yet .However, I have motivation and patience. I believe that one day, I will be the person people look up to.
